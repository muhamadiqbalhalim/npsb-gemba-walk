Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Gemba Walk? +

A Gemba Walk is a management tool used to observe actual work processes, engage with employees, gain knowledge about the work process, and explore opportunities for continuous improvement.

How are winners selected? +

Winners are selected at the end of the event based on the highest accumulated points on the leaderboard. Ties are broken by whoever completed their routes the fastest.

What happens if I scan a checkpoint out of order? +

The system will reject the scan and notify you to proceed to the correct previous checkpoint. You will not lose points, but you cannot progress until the sequence is corrected.
